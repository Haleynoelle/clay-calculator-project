Clay Calculator link

Originally a web development portfolio project, Clay Calculator has grown into a valuable resource for ceramic artists. It offers free, easy-to-use digital tools that help artists translate ideas into tangible pieces. From calculating custom clay recipes to calculating the question of how to price handmade ceramics, these tools are built with practicality and efficiency in mind, making studio work smoother and more intuitive.

Clay House link

A home for Notion templates designed to optimize artist workflows, Clay House helps ceramicists stay organized and focused on their craft. These modular templates cater to both solo artists and studio teams, covering everything from networking and community building to behind-the-scenes business management. Downloaded by 110+ artists, Clay House is an evolving ecosystem of resources supporting both the creative and professional sides of pottery.
