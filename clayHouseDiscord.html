Clay House: A Discord Server for Ceramic Artists

**this server is a work in progress and is basically in hibernation mode, but feel free to join early so you're there when it's booted up <3**


A community created for ceramicists, by a ceramicist, focused on making the art of ceramics more accessible to everyone—especially those who maybe don’t have a local community, prefer to work alone yet still want a sense of community, or can’t justify the costs of traditional studio spaces and supplies. Whatever the reason may be, this is a space to share free resources, trade materials, and support each other in the journey of learning and practicing this craft. This is a space for connection and encouraging, not a marketplace or a place for spamming content. The goal is to lower the barriers to entry for ceramics and to connect with people within and outside of our locality without the pressure to spend or make a profit.

How You Can Use the Clay House Discord:

  • Share Materials & Tools: Trade or give away tools and materials in the #free-tools-material-trades channel to make ceramics more accessible.

  • Ask for Help: Post your questions or challenges in the #general-discussion channel and get advice from experienced ceramicists.

  • Specialized Channels: Connect with others working in the same medium by joining #porcelain-creators, #stoneware-creators, or #earthenware-creators channels.

  • Collaborate on Projects: Find partners for collaboration or get feedback on your current projects in the community channels.

  • Live Feedback Sessions: Start a live video call with fellow ceramicists in voice channels for real-time advice and feedback on your building techniques.

  • Learn & Share Resources: Access a variety of tutorials, guides, and tips in the #resources channel to improve your craft.
