Cost to Fire a Kiln Calculator

Cost to Fire a Kiln Calculator

Use this calculator to determine the cost of firing a kiln by inputting the kiln's specifications and energy usage. Provide your cost per kilowatt hour, the kiln's wattage rating (or multiply volts x amps and input this number into the kiln watts rating field), the adjustment factor, and the hours of firing. Click submit to view the total cost. This tool offers a clear idea of your firing expense, helping you to focus on creating without unexpected costs.
