Firing Fee Calculator

When firing your ceramic projects, studios may charge you by weight, cubic inches, or square inches. This calculator allows you to easily figure out the costs involved. Choose the measurement unit you need, provide the relevant information (using 0 for any non-applicable fields), and press submit to get your cost. This tool streamlines the process of calculating your costs, freeing up a little more time for you to focus on your work.
