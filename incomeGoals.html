Income Goal Calculator

This calculator offers a simplified way to map out your income goals. This calculator is simply a foundational guide that you can use to gain some inspiration to create a more personalized and sustainable financial plan for your craft if you decide that you want to make a profit from your work.

Reminder that not all hobbies need to result in profit. We're allowed to pursue things simply for the sake of finding joy in it.
