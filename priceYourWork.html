Ceramics Pricing Calculator

This tool is designed to help ceramicists determine fair and profitable prices for their work by taking into account various factors that contribute to the overall cost and value of your ceramic pieces, including:

⊡ Material costs (clay, glazes, etc.)
⊡ Labor time and your desired hourly rate
⊡ Overhead costs (studio rent, utilities, equipment depreciation)
⊡ Desired profit margin

By considering these factors, this calculator helps you set prices that not only cover your costs, but also reflect the true value of your artistic work and craftsmanship.

This calculator provides a suggested retail price, you should also consider market factors, your target audience, and the uniqueness of you & your work when making final pricing decisions.
