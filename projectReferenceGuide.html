A Reference Guide for Getting Started

This reference guide was designed to help jump-start the creation process. It provides standard dimensions, weight, wall thickness, and estimated clay amounts needed for various ceramic items. It serves as a helpful starting point when you're trying to figure out what size a project needs to be and how much clay you'll need for said project.

Click on any item name and the relevant information will appear below.The data is based on typical sizes for porcelain, stoneware, and earthenware, allowing you to adjust how much clay you need according to your preferred material.
Make sure to consider your clay's shrink rate before building!
