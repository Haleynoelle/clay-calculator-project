A Reference Guide for Getting Started

If you have questions like…

⊡ What’s the standard size of a serving bowl?
⊡ What’s the recommended wall thickness for different projects?
⊡ How much porcelain is needed for a coffee mug?
⊡ How much stoneware is needed for a tea pot?
⊡ I'm in a creative rut, what should I create today?

This guide is here to answer that! It provides standard dimensions, weight, wall thickness, and estimated clay amounts for various ceramic pieces. Whether you're planning a new project or refining your process, this reference serves as a reliable starting point to determine the right size and amount of clay needed.



Click on any project name to see detailed information pop-up below. The data includes typical sizes for porcelain, stoneware, and earthenware, so you can adjust your clay amounts based on your preferred material.

Tip: Don't forget to factor in clay shrinkage! Use the Dimensions Calculator before you start building.
