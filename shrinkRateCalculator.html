Dimensions Calculator

When working on your ceramic projects, knowing which dimensions to build to for your desired outcome is crucial. This calculator factors in clay shrinkage to tell you the dimensions you need to build your project. Enter the width, height, and length that you want your final piece to be then input the shrink rate percentage. Click submit to see the dimensions you need to build your project to in its plastic stage to achieve the perfect size after firing. Focus more on your creative work and less on guesswork.
