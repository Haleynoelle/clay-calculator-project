Dimensions Calculator

This calculator helps you easily determine how your clay projects will change in size based on your clay's current stage and shrink rate. By inputting the dimensions of a piece in its fired or plastic stage, this tool calculates the equivalent size in the other form. This allows you to accurately plan for size changes, ensuring your work turns out just the way you envision it to.
