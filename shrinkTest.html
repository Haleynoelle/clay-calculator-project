Shrink Rate Calculator

Accurately measuring clay shrinkage is essential for achieving the desired final dimensions of your ceramic projects. This calculator helps you determine the shrinkage rate by comparing dimensions at different stages. Enter the dimension (either width, length, or height) of your project in its plastic clay stage and the dimension after it has been fired (glazeware clay stage). Click submit to calculate the shrinkage rate. This tool provides valuable insights, ensuring your projects come out just the way you intend, allowing you to focus on creating perfect ceramic pieces.
