Volume Calculator

Knowing the volume of your ceramics is useful for both you, the creator, and the end user. For ceramicists, accurate volume measurements can help ensure proper material usage. For those that end up using the piece regularly, knowing the volume helps in assessing the functionality and capacity of the piece. This volume calculator assists in determining the volume of various shapes, including rectangles, cylinders, frustums, hemispheres, and ellipsoids. Be sure to measure from the inside of your project for precise calculations. Enter the necessary dimensions for your shape and click submit to get the volume.
